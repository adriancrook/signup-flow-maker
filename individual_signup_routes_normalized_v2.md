## 1. The Gatekeeper

* **Screen:** Select User Role
  * "How will you use Typing.com?" (Student, Parent, or Adult)
    * I'm a student
    * I'm a parent/homeschool teacher, setting up an account for my child
    * I'm an adult, learning for myself
  * **Student:**
    * **Screen:** "Do you have a class code from your teacher?"
      * **Yes:** Redirect to "Join Class" flow.
      * **No:** Redirect to standard individual student signup.

## 2. Student (Non-Classroom)

* **Affirmation:** Social Proof 
  * "Great choice. You are joining 50 million students, parents, and teachers who trust Typing.com."
* **Screen: Purpose Assessment**
  * "Why are you looking to improve your typing today?"
    * **Options:** [Success in School/College], [Tech & Coding Interests], [Just for Fun & Games], [Personal Growth].
    * **Affirmation:** "Great goal. Many students use our premium coding and test prep units to get ahead."
* **Screen:** Mini Typing Test
  * "Let's get a baseline. Type the sentence below:"
  * *Box displays: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog."*
  * User types -> System calculates WPM.
* **Affirmation:** Project Growth
  * "You clocked in at [X] WPM. We can 2x that speed with practice."

  * "What is your target typing speed?"
  * **Options:** [Speed Goal Selection].
  * **Affirmation:** "We've got you. We'll track your progress every step of the way to ensure you hit that goal."
* **Screen:** Barriers to Practice
  * "What usually stops you from practicing?"
  * **Options:** [Distractions/Ads], [Boredom], [Lack of Time]
  * **Affirmation:** "We get it. That's why PLUS removes all video ads to keep you focused."
* **Screen:** Select Motivation 
  * "What keeps you motivated when you're learning?"
  * **Options:** [Streaks], [Games], [Progress Tracking]
  * **Affirmation:** "Typing.com uses gamified learning to keep you engaged."
* **Screen: Discovery**
  * "Where did you hear about us?"
  * **Options:** [Teacher], [Friend/Classmate], [Social Media], [Search Engine].
* **Screen: Daily Practice Goal** 
  * "How much time can you commit to practicing each day?"
  * **Options:** [5 Mins], [15 Mins], [30 Mins].
  * **Affirmation:** "Daily Goal tracker activated. Consistency is the secret to doubling your speed."
